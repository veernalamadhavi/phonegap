About Us

SundayKart is bringing an easy to use ecommerce platform that connects consumers with their trusted grocery retailers, who are serving them for many years.

This ecommerce platform is elegantly crafted to present an effortless online shopping experience to the consumer and an easy to manage ecommerce solution for the grocery retailer.

Customers are given the benefit to organize shop and place order within minutes from the comfort of their home. All purchases are delivered to them by their retailer in a matter of hours and payment is made at the time of delivery.

Grocery retailers are given the benefit to manage and market their products online in a matter of minutes. Retailers have the ability to broadcast specials and discounts to all their customers. With SundayKart proprietary rating technology, promotes the best retailer to the new customers.

As a desire to bring effortless shopping experience, cost effective and easy manageable ecommerce solution, every day SundayKart strives to be leader in providing easy to use ecommerce platform.
